CREME BRULEE

Thermomix version. Serves 4-6. Store in fridge wrapped. *Note: need to experiment with temperature and cooking method.

INGREDIENTS
Ingredient Amount
Vanilla 1 Tbsp
Full Cream Milk 250g
Egg Yolks 5 Yolks
Caster Sugar 70g (+ To Serve)
Pouring/Whipping Cream 250g
EQUIPMENT
Equipment
Thermomix
Thermomix Butterfly Whisk
Kitchen Blowtorch
Ramekins (per Serving)
METHOD
  1. Preheat oven to 135° C fan forced*. Place 4-6 ramekins into a deep baking dish and fill half-way with water to act as a bain-marie for the ramekins.

  2. Place 5 egg yolks and 70g sugar into the Thermomix bowl. Mix for 10 secs/speed 3.

  3. Insert the butterfly whisk into the bowl. Add 250g cream, then mix for 15 secs/speed 3.

  4. Separately, mix the 250g milk and 1 tbsp vanilla together, then heat until the mixture is lukewarm. Add the milk mixture to the bowl and mix for 20 secs/speed 3.

  5. Pour the mixture into the ramekins, then place in the oven and bake for apr. 45 minutes at 135° C* or until set. The mixture is "set" when the mixture jiggles when shaken, but isn't liquidy.

  6. Set aside to cool slightly, then place ramekins in the fridge for at least 6 hours, but preferably overnight.

  7. To serve, sprinkle sugar on top of the creme brulee and caramelise it using a blow torch before serving.
